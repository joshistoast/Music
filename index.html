Variations of Rock

What is Rock?

Rock music is a broad genre of popular music that originated as "rock and roll" in the United States in the early 1950s, and developed into a range of different styles in the 1960s and later, particularly in the United Kingdom and in the United States

Alternative Rock

A genre of rock music that emerged from the independent music underground of the 1980s and became widely popular by the 1990s.

In this instance, the word Alternative, refers to the genres destinction from mainstream rock music.

Heavy Metal

Heavy metal is traditionally characterized by loud distorted guitars, emphatic rhythms, dense bass-and-drum sound, and vigorous vocals. Metal subgenres variously emphasize, alter, or omit one or more of these attributes.

Popular Artists

Iron Maiden

Metallica
